Real-World Examples

DIDs are starting to be used in a bunch of areas like digital wallets, healthcare, schools, and more. Like, instead of carrying around a paper diploma, a school could give you a digital one linked to your DID to prove you graduated.

Another thing is you could log into websites without needing a password. You’d just use your DID to prove it’s really you. It’s safer and more convenient.

Even big companies and the government are looking into using DIDs for stuff like secure voting, travel, and digital ID cards. It’s still relatively new, but it could change a lot in the future.
